About Me

Hello World! My name is Jo, a self-taught front-end web developer. My interest in development stemmed from an internship experience where I saw a volunteer changing an html span tag. I thought to myself "Why can't I do that?". So I went home and started learning HTML. I was a junior in college at the time, majoring in Political Economy, and it was too late to change my major to Computer Science. Plus, I really enjoyed my major anyway. I continued learning front-end web development on my own and eventually received a scholarship to attend a local coding bootcamp. In the very near future, I would like to expand my skills enought to venture into Full Stack Web development by learning C# and back-end Javascript. I am also interested in learning more about Technical Product Management as I find the intersection of UX, Tech and Business to be very engaging and dynamic.
