About Me

Hello World! My name is Jo, a self-taught front-end web developer.
My interest in development stemmed from an internship experience where I saw a volunteer changing an html span tag. I thought to myself "Why can't I do that?". So I went home and started learning HTML. I was a junior in college at the time, majoring in Political Economy, and it was too late to change my major to Computer Science. Plus, I really enjoyed my major anyway. I continued learning front-end web development on my own and eventually received a scholarship to attend a local coding bootcamp. In the very near future, I would like to expand my skills enought to venture into Full Stack Web development by learning C# and full stack Javascript. In my spare time I enjoy playing tennis, pool and lounging by the Boise River in beautiful Idaho.
